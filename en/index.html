[Українською]
Automatic transliteration of Cyrillic text into Latin on web pages.
Switch Latinization system in the popup window.
More Latinization projects are in the extension settings. Mark the ones that should be visible in the popup.
Share your favorite Latinization system on social networks. Use the short link.
Create your own Latinization system. Just start editing the one you like in the extension settings.
Add to the blacklist those sites where transliteration should be disabled.
Or just turn off that site in the popup.
Alternatively, use the whitelist of sites and disable transliteration on all other sites.
Use the input screen to transliterate text.
